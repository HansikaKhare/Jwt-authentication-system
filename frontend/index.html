Quick Test Guide

  1. Open Login Page Click the "Login Page" card above or navigate to login.html
  2. Register New User Click "Register here" and fill in:
    • Username: testuser
    • Email: test@example.com
    • Password: password123
  3. Auto-Login & Redirect After registration, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your profile page
  4. View Profile See your user information including ID, username, email, and role
  5. Test Logout Click "Logout" button - you'll be redirected to login page
  6. Login Again Use your credentials to login and verify JWT token handling
  7. Inspect JWT Token Press F12 → Application → Local Storage → Find jwt_token
    Decode it at jwt.io
